Selected Robotics class projects

Overview

     For one of my classes in the fall semester of my freshman year, we did many lego robotics projects that I thought were cool, and the ones I think were the best I put brief descriptions of below​​

Robotic arm

A lego robotic arm that picked up  ping pong balls from one side and dropped into the other using the wheel "grippers." Used two motors to grip the ball and one to flip it from one side to the other.

Astronaut Drill

A lego drill designed to remove a nut from a pole and place it again with design considerations of an astronaut. Won the in-class competition for speed.

Monkey-esq Robot

A lego robotic "monkey" designed to, well, behave like a monkey as a part of a biomimicry project. Used motors on the side to move "arms" up and down an a back motor to move both arms back and forth. Originally planned to have it climb a rope, but the legos weren't strong enough

Halloween Game

A halloween themed game group project. It was designed for a robotics haunted house in the campus makespace. It was a reaction time based game which "cut" your hand off for losing and gave you candy if you won.
